Research Highlights

Core directions spanning probabilistic hardware, optimization, and efficient AI systems.

p-bit and Stochastic Computing

Energy-efficient architectures based on probabilistic bits and stochastic representations for scalable computation.

Simulated Annealing and Quantum-Inspired Optimization

Fast-converging optimization methods for combinatorial problems, including Ising model solvers and related hardware acceleration.

Few-shot Learning and AI Systems

Efficient learning systems for real-world deployment, with attention to edge and hardware-aware AI applications.
