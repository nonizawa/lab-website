Research

Our research integrates algorithms and hardware implementations on FPGA, GPU, and ASIC platforms.

Probabilistic Computing

We study p-bit based systems and stochastic computing for energy-efficient and scalable computation.

Optimization Algorithms

We develop simulated annealing and quantum-inspired optimization methods for solving complex combinatorial problems.

AI Systems

We explore few-shot learning and efficient AI models for real-world and edge applications.

Algorithm and Hardware Co-Design

We emphasize the full stack of research, from probabilistic algorithms and optimization methods to practical implementation on modern computing platforms.

Open Research Outputs

Publications, arXiv papers, and open-source code are part of our research culture, helping students connect theory, experimentation, and reproducible implementation.
