Join Us

We are looking for motivated students who are interested in probabilistic computing, optimization, and AI systems.

Research Environment

International students are highly encouraged to apply.

We offer opportunities to work on both algorithms and hardware implementations.

Research supervision is available in English.

We Are Looking for Students Interested In

  • Probabilistic computing and p-bit systems
  • Stochastic optimization and simulated annealing
  • AI and few-shot learning
  • Hardware implementation using FPGA, GPU, or ASIC

Example Research Topics

  • Design of p-bit based probabilistic systems
  • Acceleration of simulated annealing on GPU/FPGA
  • Hardware implementation of stochastic computing
  • Few-shot learning for edge AI systems

How to Apply

Please send an email including:

  • Your CV
  • Brief description of your research interests
  • Relevant experience (if any)

Contact

If you are interested in joining the lab, please contact us by email with a brief introduction and your application materials.
